arts grooves & foods is an event hosted by Spark SC on March 12, 2015 at Tutor Campus Center Ballroom USC. Arts, Grooves, & Foods is a celebration of creativity, innovation, and initiative what we believe to be three powerful forces of culture. This festival shines the spotlight on USC students as well as the makers of the entrepreneurial community.
